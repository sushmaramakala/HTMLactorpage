VIJAY

Joseph Vijay Chandrasekhar (born 22 June 1974),[2][3] known professionally as Vijay, is an Indian actor and singer who works mainly in Tamil cinema.[4][5] He is among the highest paid actors in India[6][7] and has featured in Forbes India's Celebrity 100 list on seven occasions.[8][9] He has played the lead in 67 films.
